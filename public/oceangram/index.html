The problem

Your Telegram is broken

Not the app. The paradigm. One session, one window, infinite noise.

🔒

Trapped in one tab

Your Telegram lives in a single browser tab or desktop app. Switch context? Start scrolling. There's no ambient layer — it's all or nothing.

🤖

Invisible to your AI

Your coding agent can't see your conversations. It can't read requirements from chat, post updates, or act on messages. Communication is siloed from your tools.

🔔

500 notifications, 5 matter

Every group, every channel, every spam bot — they all compete for your attention equally. There's no filter for what actually matters to you.

Oceangram Tray

Ambient messaging.
Zero distraction.

A Mac menu bar app. Whitelist who matters. Their avatar appears as a floating bubble. Click, reply, close, done.

  • ✓ Whitelist-only — Choose your VIPs. Everyone else stays silent.
  • ✓ Floating bubbles — Avatars appear on your screen edge. Glanceable. Unobtrusive.
  • ✓ Popup chat — Click a bubble → minimal chat window. Reply → close → back to work.
  • ✓ Menu bar native — Lives in your Mac menu bar. No dock icon. No window management.
  • ✓ Keyboard shortcuts — ⌘+Shift+T to toggle. ⌘+Enter to send. Esc to dismiss.
